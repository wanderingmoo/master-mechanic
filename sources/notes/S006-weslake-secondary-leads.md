## Why this matters

The source connects Harry Weslake/Weslake Engineering, Dan Gurney, and Gurney-Weslake cylinder-head work. It is a useful pointer for the Gurney-Weslake 302 package but not a service specification.

## Extracted leads

- Weslake is described as providing Gurney-Weslake cylinder heads for Gulf-Wyer Ford GT40 Mk I engines associated with the 1968 and 1969 Le Mans wins.
- The source points toward stronger references including books on Cosworth/Ford power, Gurney's Eagles, and Ford vs. Ferrari histories.

## Use constraints

- Do not treat the presence of Gurney-Weslake heads as verified until the installed heads are inspected and matched to build records.
- Do not infer valve sizes, chamber volume, port work, compression ratio, plug type, lash, or camshaft data from this source.
