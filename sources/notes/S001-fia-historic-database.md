## Why this source matters

S001 is the root FIA Historic Database path used to locate FIA historic car records, regulations, Period Appendix J material, homologation-list material, and Appendix K material.

This source is a source-control and routing path. It is not a vehicle service manual, does not identify the project car, and does not make any settings actionable.

## Extracted facts

- The page is titled "FIA Historic Database."
- The homepage exposes a car search by make, range, model, year, or homologation number.
- The homepage links to Cars, Regulations, and the HTP List.
- The homepage links users to Regulations, Period Appendix J, Period Regulations, Homologation List, and Appendix K.
- The homepage states that, for any homologated car, HTP use requires an ASN-certified copy of the original or retrospective homologation form.
- The homepage states that forms available on the website are for consultation only and will not be recognised as valid at an event.
