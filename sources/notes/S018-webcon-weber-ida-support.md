## Why this source matters

The current engine/induction file treats Weber IDA-style induction as a research placeholder. S018 gives a more authoritative path for identifying Weber IDA components, service kits, diagrams, and tuning/service literature than generic web references.

This source does not prove that the project car has Weber IDA carburetors installed, does not identify the exact carburetor series, and does not provide a GT40/Gurney-Weslake 302 jetting specification.

## Mechanic-facing implications

- Use S018 to source Weber IDA diagrams, service kits, rebuild kits, jets, and related support parts after the installed carburetor model and series are documented.
- Use the Webcon product codes as ordering leads only after confirming the installed carburetor body and exact service-kit applicability.
- Do not use generic 48 IDA assumptions to set jetting, float level, fuel pressure, idle mixture, accelerator-pump shot, or linkage synchronization on the project car.
- For a Gurney-Weslake 302, carburetor settings remain dependent on actual carburetor model, choke size, jet stack, cam, compression, fuel, exhaust, and dyno/road-test evidence.
