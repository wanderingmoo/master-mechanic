## Why this matters

S015 verifies a base Ford GT 40 homologation entry for a ZF manual 5-speed transaxle, but the installed transaxle is unknown and no ZF 5DS-25 service manual is stored locally. S008 gives the project an official ZF acquisition path for technical information, lubricant lists, catalog/partner lookup, transmission service, and support before any ZF-specific mechanic setting is considered.

## Extracted facts

- ZF identifies itself as a global supplier of mobility products and systems, including product and aftermarket offerings.
- ZF Aftermarket exposes official catalog paths for technical information, lubricants, data sheets, partner finding, and transmission product/service information.
- ZF Aftermarket states that logged-in users can access additional technical information.
- ZF Aftermarket states that its lubricant lists are used to find the right oil for a given ZF application.
- ZF Aftermarket describes passenger-car transmission service, genuine spare parts, service-center network, and partner-finder paths.

## Extraction boundary

This source note does not capture a ZF 5DS-25 manual, GT40-specific ZF oil specification, final-drive setup, backlash, preload, torque values, shift-linkage settings, or ratio data. It also does not prove the project car has a ZF transaxle installed.
