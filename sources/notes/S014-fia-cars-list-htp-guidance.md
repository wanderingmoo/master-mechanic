## Why this matters

The FIA cars-list page explains how homologation documents are treated for historic technical passport use. This is a source-control rule, not a GT40 specification.

## Extracted facts

- For a homologated car, the FIA page says the HTP must be accompanied by an ASN-certified copy of the original Homologation Form or Retrospective Homologation Form.
- The page states that forms available on the website are for consultation and are not recognized as valid on an event.

## Mechanic-facing implications

- A downloaded or viewed form can support shop research, but event eligibility needs a certified copy through the appropriate ASN channel.
- The knowledge base should distinguish research copies from event-valid documents.
- The Ford GT40 Mk I entry is captured as S015; event eligibility still requires an ASN-certified copy under the FIA guidance.
