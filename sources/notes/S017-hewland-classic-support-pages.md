## Why this source matters

S015 records a 1968 Ford GT 40 homologation evolution for a Hewland LG600 5-speed gearbox. S016 is the local archived LG500/LG600 manual. S017 documents the support context around Hewland Classic: licensing relationship, historic gearbox support, gearbox-range coverage, manual availability, and contact path.

This source does not prove the project car has a Hewland LG600 installed. It supports source authority and future support routing only after the installed transaxle is identified.

## Extracted facts

- Hewland Classic describes Hewland Engineering Limited as having announced the trading of Hewland Classic Limited.
- Hewland Classic states it operates through a long-term licensing partnership with Hewland Engineering for historic racing-market support.
- Hewland Classic says it services the spares market for Hewland gearboxes from the 1960s to the 1990s.
- The gearbox range page lists LG500 and LG600 among supported gearbox types.
- The manuals page lists an LG500/LG600 manual and warns that original manuals may have updates or revisions from original design.
- The manuals page includes motorsport-use and maintenance warnings, including that parts and gearboxes are mechanical devices subject to wear, fatigue, disintegration, collision damage, or improper-use damage.
- Hewland Classic provides contact routing at sales@hewlandclassic.com and +44 1977 615455.

## Mechanic-facing implications

- Use S017 to justify treating S016 as a credible Hewland Classic support/manual route for LG500/LG600 work.
