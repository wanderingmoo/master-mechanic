## What was inspected

- The Henry Ford Remote Research Services page.
- The Benson Ford Research Center about page for current public-access and inquiry context.

## Extracted facts

- The Henry Ford offers fee-based remote research for off-site researchers when a reading-room visit is not possible.
- The service is for in-depth research into archival, library, and in some cases artifact collections; it is not a general research assistant service.
- Example uses include researching archival materials for specific topics, providing further information on accession or folder contents, folder page counts for duplication, and in-depth information on specific artifacts.
- The page states that paying for remote research does not guarantee the requested information will be found.
- The page points researchers to contact `research.center@thehenryford.org`.
- Duplication options include document, image, audiovisual, production record, digital image, and part drawing order forms.
- The Benson Ford Research Center page states that public in-person research is closed until further notice but staff are responding to inquiries.
